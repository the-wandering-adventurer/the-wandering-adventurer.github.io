Brynn#

Brynn is a druid from the Druidic College of Galdir and the protagonist of our story.

Attributes#

1 2 1 2 3

Starting Assets#

Sighted#

Brynn can sense the magical world much like a person hears, sees and smells. Druidic scholars believe that all people are born with this ability, but because humans are non-magical by nature, most can’t recognize it and even fewer can master it.

Augur#

Brynn can contact a flock of crows to ask a question or obtain information. Crows have a unique connection to the mystical forces of nature, giving them an awareness of the world that humans don’t have. Whether they deign to impart this knowledge to Brynn is another matter.

Awakening#

Brynn can attempt to call on the natural spirits of the world to summon an entity to aid him. The spirits are fickle, however, and often have an agenda of their own.

Obtained Assets#

Fated#

Brynn wants nothing to do with prophecies, but they seem to want to do something with him.
