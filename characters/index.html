Characters

January 2, 2025 · 1 min

This page contains a list of the characters that appear in the story, along with a short description of who they are.

  • Arasen - a servant of Kodroth. Later becomes a bonewalker in the Far Forest
  • Brynn - the main protagonist of the story. Trained as a Druid of Galdir
  • Dorosi - an elven Scout. Afraid of druids
  • Edda - the kindly Shaman of Brightmyst. The village’s healer
  • Fanir - Master Druid in Galdir who teaches students how to tame demons
  • Kodroth - a raider from the Clan Isles
  • Kynan - a High Druid in Galdir
  • Mattissa - an elven Scout
  • Nessana - an elven Scout Leader
  • Rhoddri - the stern Mayor of Brightmyst and former Knight of the Realm
  • Seleeku - an elven Scout in the Far Forest who first finds Brynn
  • Sibila - a High Druid in Galdir, the youngest ever to have risen to the rank
  • Themon - a High Druid in Galdir. Has a vision/prophesy of the coming Darkness
